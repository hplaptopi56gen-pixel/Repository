# AI Idea: Urdu-Pashto AI Tutor for Students

## 1. Problem
Pakistan me AI aur tech ki zyada tar taleem English me hai. 
Urdu aur Pashto bolne wale students ko samajhne me mushkil hoti hai. 
Is wajah se bohot log AI seekhna chor dete hain.

## 2. Proposed AI Solution
Ek AI Tutor banayenge jo Elements of AI ke concepts ko Urdu aur Pashto me asaan lafzon me samjhaye.
- Local examples use karega: kheti, business, taleem
- Sawal ka jawab text aur voice dono me dega

Prototype ke liye hum GPT/Gemini API use kar sakte hain.

## 3. Data Needed
- Elements of AI ka Urdu/Pashto translation
- Students ke common sawal aur jawab
- AI terms ki Urdu dictionary

## 4. Potential Impact
- 220M+ Urdu speakers aur 50M+ Pashto speakers ke liye AI education easy ho jayegi
- Pakistan me AI literacy barhegi
- Students aur teachers apne kaam me AI use kar payenge

## 5. Next Steps
Phase 1: Simple website chatbot
Phase 2: Voice support + Pashto add karna
Phase 3: Free mobile app
